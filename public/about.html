ABOUT ME

I'm an artist turned iOS engineer,
movie enthusiast, thrill seeker, and
most importantly an Imagine Dragons
Fan. I've spent half my life in Idaho
Falls, and the other half in Utah.

As a child I loved building forts in
the trees and ditches, and now I love
building inspiring features with
Swift. I love sharing my creativity
with the world to hopefully make it
brighter.

On my days off you will probably find
me either working on my latest
artwork, playing with my six-year-
old nephew, or hanging out with my
beautiful wife who I love more than
anything (even Imagine Dragons if
you can believe it).
